1. Bubble charts : Which State has most Collisions ?

The first graph shows drivers involved in total collisions per billion miles in each state, The red circles shows the ones with the most collisions

Further,we can split the states that have more speeding collisions & Alcohol Impaired collisions out of total collisions. This will tell us which State has more Speeding drivers & Alcohol Impaired Collisions

North Dakota and South Carolina have most Collisons compared to Other states

Hawaii and Pennsylvania have a greater percentage of Speeding drivers compared to Other states,despite having lesser total Collisions
